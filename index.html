About:

I am a PhD candidate and a Teaching Fellow in the Department of Mathematics at Boston University. I completed my B.A. in mathematics with a minor in computer science at the University of Chicago.

I am interested in number theory with an inclination towards algebraic number theory.

You can also find me in my office at Boston University's MCS B27F.

News and Updates

August 2020. I am organizing the BU Number Theory Expository Seminar (BUNTES) for the fall semester. The topic is the theory of Complex Multiplication. The goal will be to cover the following topics: CM for elliptic curves, CM for abelian varieties, Kronecker's Jugendtraum for CM fields, Galois Representations arising from abelian varieties with CM.
